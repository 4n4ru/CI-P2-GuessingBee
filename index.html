How to play

Play the classic Hangman game, but with a twist!
Today, no-one gets hanged. Instead we are going to try to help some bees. Save the flowers to make the bees happy.
Pick a difficulty, easy for up to 8, normal up to 6, and hard up to 4 errors.
The game is played by picking letters and trying to guess the phrase before you run out of moves.
Good luck!
